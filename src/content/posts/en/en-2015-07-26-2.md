There was an exchange like this.

> **Writing a novel is not an everyday act**

> Murakami: So even when I am aware that it is a day off, if I think maybe I will write today, or I have to write, I end up writing. And basically, I do not like writing novels.

> Ota: You do not like it?

> Murakami: I do not like it, but it is the thing that gives me the greatest sense of fulfillment. There is no greater fulfillment than what I get by doing it. And there is no other path where I can earn more money than that, so I do it. It is not in the category of liking.
